Ramadan.

(01) The brief

A mall is a destination, not a shortcut.

Every mall in Cairo runs a Ramadan campaign. The challenge for East View was to feel less like another promoted venue and more like a place you actually want to be during the season — for the bazaar, for the F&B, for the vibe.

The plan covered both the feed and the stories: Ramadan Kareem moments, tenant partnerships (Mousa Seafood, others), bazaar activations, and the ongoing "Shop. Dine. Enjoy." positioning the mall carries year-round — just turned up for the season.

(02) The execution

Make every tenant feel like the main event.

Instead of one blanket mall campaign, we built a content plan that let each tenant partner shine in their own moment. Mousa Seafood got "A Different Kind of Iftar." The bazaar got its own voice — "stroll, discover, enjoy." The mall itself held the umbrella.

Each post had a specific type (static, reel, carousel), a defined TOV, and a caption crafted for the moment. Over the course of the month, the plan laddered from "Ramadan is here" energy to specific destination-driving content to end-of-season gratitude.
